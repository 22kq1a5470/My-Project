Andhra sweets are known for their unique flavors, textures, and ingredients: Ingredients
Andhra sweets often use jaggery instead of sugar, which gives them a caramel-like flavor.
Sesame seeds are also common, adding a nutty flavor and crunchy texture. Textures
Andhra sweets can have a variety of textures, including crunchy, layered, or melt-in-your-mouth.
Flavors Andhra sweets offer a wide range of flavors, from rich and nutty to syrupy. Traditional recipes
Andhra sweets are made using traditional recipes that have been passed down through generations. Cultural significance
